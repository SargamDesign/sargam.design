Sargam Design

A methodology for creating strong and scalable design systems based on the aesthetics of Indian Classical Music.

From the design foundations, process, voice and tone, to conceptual models, style-guide, patterns, and documentation. Sargam brings all of these together in a way that allow design teams to learn, build, and iterate fast, and grow. The system that gives flexibility for working with the unknowns of the future and drive to scale companies.
